✉️ A Message from the Founder

When I created Faith4Penpals, I had one simple vision — to build a free place where Christians from all over the world could meet, connect, and encourage one another in faith.

So many pen pal sites out there are focused on making money, charging fees just to send a message or find a friend. I didn't want that. I believe Christian community should be open to everyone, regardless of their financial situation.

This site is — and always will be — completely free.

However, keeping a website running does have costs. If you have been blessed by this community and would like to help keep it going, I humbly ask that you consider either buying the founder's merch or buying us a coffee. Every little bit helps and is deeply appreciated.

But please — never feel pressured. Just being here and connecting with fellow believers is enough. 🙏

— The Founder of Faith4Penpals
